ABOUT US

GECI

Government Engineering College Idukki came into existence in 2000 with the insight of becoming a pool of expertise in technical education along with the commitments to bring out promising young professionals who resonates social values. For the past 20 years, this prodigious institution has succeeded in transforming the minds of engineering aspirants by enriching and nurturing young minds a better tomorrow.

ADVAYA

Government Engineering College Idukki is back with the 9th edition of ADVAYA- the annual national level techno-cultural fest, from 14th to 16th February 2020. ‘ADVAYA’ as the name depicts, is a united effort to come up with pioneering ideas for technical excellence. Advaya portrays its legacy through a coordinated fusion of technology and culture. A total of 80 events including technical workshops, departmental exhibitions, proshows and games are featured in Advaya challenging the intellect of the participants and excite the minds of the audience.
